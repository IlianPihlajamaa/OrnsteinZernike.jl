OrnsteinZernike.fourier! — Method
fourier!(F̂, F, plan, equation::OZEquation{3, T1, T2, T3}) where {T1, T2, T3}

computes the three dimensional radial fourier transform of F = rf(r), returning F̂ = kf̂(k), where f̂ is the fourier transform of f. it uses the discrete sine tranform provided by the r2r function of FFTW internally.
